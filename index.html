# Privacy Policy for WorkyDay WorkyDay does not collect, store or share personal data from users. The app provides information about workplaces, visa requirements and locations in Australia. Some features may open external links (email, phone, websites or social media) which are managed by third-party services. WorkyDay may use map services such as OpenStreetMap to display locations. No personal user data is stored on our servers. If you have any questions, contact: hello@workyday
