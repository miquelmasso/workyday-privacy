Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-05 · Contact: hello@workyday.com

WorkyDay (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the WorkyDay app (the “App”) handles information when you use it.

1) What the App does

The App helps users find workplaces and job-related information across Australia and provides guidance related to the Working Holiday Visa (WHV) journey.

2) Information we collect

We do not require you to create an account to read public information in the App.

  • Information you provide voluntarily: if you contact us by email, we receive the content of your message and your email address.
  • Approximate location (optional): if you allow location access, the App can show nearby workplaces. You can disable location at any time in your device settings.
  • Device/diagnostic data (optional): basic crash logs may be provided by your operating system to improve stability.

3) How we use information

  • To provide App functionality (e.g., showing nearby places when location is enabled).
  • To respond to support requests.
  • To improve reliability and fix bugs.

4) Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only in these cases:

  • Service providers: limited sharing may occur for hosting and app infrastructure.
  • Legal reasons: if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

5) Data retention

We keep support emails as long as needed to handle your request and maintain records for reasonable operational purposes. If you want us to delete your support email history, contact us.

6) Your choices

  • You can disable location permission at any time in your device settings.
  • You can contact us to request deletion of messages you sent us.

7) Children’s privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8) Security

We use reasonable measures to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the “Effective date” above when changes are made.

10) Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at hello@workyday.com.
